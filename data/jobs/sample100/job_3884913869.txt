General Summary…. The Parcel Manifest Specialist ensures the accurate, timely, and controlled mail manifesting process is completed starting with inbound parcel induction, to out-loading parcel shipments for insertion into the postal stream for delivery. They will Exercise utmost care handling and processing parcels to ensure timely, accurate tendering to the USPS or third party carrier within 12 hours of manifesting at the site.
