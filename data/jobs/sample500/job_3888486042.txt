Title: Head of HSEQ, Cement
Work Type: Full-time
Description: Head of HSEQ, FLSmidth Cement Responsible for the development of strategies, standards, and policies for health, safety and environmental aspects to offer the highest quality on a global basis in cooperation with FLSmidth Cement Management Team.
