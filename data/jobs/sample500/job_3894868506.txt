Title: Office Services Site Manager
Experience Level: Mid-Senior level
Work Type: Full-time
Description: It's fun to work at a company where people truly believe in what they are doing! Job Description: The Site Manager is responsible for overseeing the daily operations and ensuring exceptional client service at one of our on-site support services centers or client sites. Will also be responsible for the supervision of all employees at the site, including recruiting, training, and development of their professional skills.
