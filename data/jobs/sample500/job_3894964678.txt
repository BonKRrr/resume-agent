Title: Family Care Manager Supervisor I - Child Welfare
Work Type: Full-time
Description: Company Description Kids Central, Inc. is a nonprofit lead agency that coordinates a community-based system of care for abused, neglected, and abandoned children and their families. The organization is committed to promoting the welfare of children through abuse/neglect prevention services, in-home care, foster care, and adoption. Kids Central serves Citrus, Hernando, Lake, Marion, and Sumter counties in Florida. Role Description This is a full-time role as a Family Care Manager Supervisor I - Child Welfare at Kids Central, Inc. located in Ocala, FL. The Family Care Manager Supervisor I will oversee and supervise a team of Family Care Managers responsible for providing comprehensive services to children and families involved in the child welfare system. Responsibilities include case management, training, and elder care.
