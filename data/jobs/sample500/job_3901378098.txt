Title: Regional Vice President - Western Region
Experience Level: Executive
Work Type: Full-time
Description: Job Summary:Reporting to the Vice President of Sales, as Regional Vice President you will provide leadership to a team of skilled Regional Sales Managers & District Sales Managers with an overall goal to maximize the sales, take market share, and increase profitability of the company. A primary job function will be coaching and developing your sales managers in the region to be more effective at coaching and developing their Sales team. You'll work with all parts of the company to ensure that field execution is adequate and consistent with both corporate and regional objectives. You’ll need to motivate and maintain your team’s focus in multiple Regions and Districts, with managers and their sales reps/territory managers who sell to our distributors, dealers, and “Target” end users in the residential and commercial construction channels. You will continually challenge the field sales team to pursue new growth opportunities through key current/new dealers and “Target” end-users to grow our influence in each market. You’ll also be required to incorporate various sales programs to provide a sell-in/sell-through sales strategy to further our market penetration. This is NOT an office job. With travel requirements up to 50%, you’ll need to successfully execute at all levels and have efficient and effective time management skills. And this includes allocating time once a month to work/ride with Account Managers (And their DSM/RSM), in the truck, from the first call of the day to the last. (Not just showing up for events or a meeting.) Your role here is to evaluate and measure our sales process with the customer, and the level of coaching that is happening daily. Email required to identify and engage executives of your largest dealers/users and spend time working closely with them to Build Makita Champions. Lastly, to be successful in this highly competitive Power Tool industry, if you’re not taking share, you’re losing share; therefore, you’ll also be required to oversee a consistent pipeline of new conversion opportunities and, most importantly, progress them into results. Working through a defined conversion process will be a fundamental KPI of your success in this position.
