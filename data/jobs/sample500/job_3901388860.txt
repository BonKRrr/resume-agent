Title: Chemistry Teacher
Work Type: Full-time
Description: Hampton Roads Academy seeks a talented, creative individual with an energetic spirit who works well collaboratively, and who values and supports the school’s overall mission. BASIC DESCRIPTION:The applicant must have a clear understanding and experience delivering an AP Chemistry, Honors Chemistry, and General Chemistry curriculum. This teacher should demonstrate a passion for chemistry and a creative enthusiasm which inspires young learners. Excellent communication skills are essential. The position also requires a professional who understands the importance of providing rigorous instruction in a nurturing environment. Any wider, flexible experience teaching other subjects and/or demonstrated interest in research would be considered an advantage for the right candidate. Essential Duties:Utilize best instructional practices, methods, materials, and technology to increase student achievement.Inspire and encourage student enthusiasm for learning.Effectively manage the classroom to foster our school’s culture which promotes high academic achievement and intellectual engagement.Teach necessary developmental skills based on the school's scope and sequence. Implement 21st century technology into classroom instruction as Hampton Roads Academy embraces a 1:1 iPad program. Work collaboratively and communicate effectively with fellow teachers as well as students’ parents.Maintain careful class records.Carefully supervise and assure student safety daily. Engage in the school community. Job Qualifications:B.S. in ChemistryAbility to work both independently and collaboratively with colleagues.Strong classroom management skills, procedures, and strategies for working with students.Strong oral and written communication skills.Enthusiasm for education and student engagement.Sense of humor.
