Title: Licensed Professional Counselor
Work Type: Contract
Description: About Us LunaJoy Health is a virtual mental health care provider designed specifically for women. Our approach to therapy, counseling, and medication management is flexible, holistic, and personalized to meet the unique needs of each individual. The company was founded in 2020 by Shama Rathi, MD and Sipra Laddha, MD who recognized the need for a health care provider focused on women's mental health. We are actively seeking partnerships with providers who share in our commitment to helping woman from all walks of life, offering support that covers the entire life span of a woman. Role DescriptionThis is an independent contractor (1099) role for a Mental Health Counselor. The counselor will conduct virtual therapy sessions with clients, develop treatment plans, provide case management, offer relationship counseling, and assess and diagnose mental health disorders. The counselor will also maintain accurate and timely documentation of all treatment services, and maintain communication with other mental health care professionals as needed.
