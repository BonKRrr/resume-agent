Tasks and Qualifications: What You Will Do In This Role Repair and maintenance of AGVs including calibrating sensors, component adjustment/replacement, component lubrication, hydraulics troubleshooting, etc. Completes preventative maintenance routines, documentation, and procedures. Use electrical test equipment to troubleshoot electrical circuitry (5V to 480V). Create and close work orders into asset management system with appropriate data to include labor hours, equipment maintenance, and parts used. Troubleshoot AGV problems and understand resources needed to resolve them. Locate and track spare parts from inventory. Performs complex maintenance and equipment testing to ensure they meet specifications. Provide assistance with training customers to operate equipment. Respond to service calls and correct equipment failures and faults. Identify problems as they occur and take appropriate steps to solve them.
