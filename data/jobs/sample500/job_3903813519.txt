Title: ED Technician--Brownwood--ED--Per Diem 12-hour Days
Experience Level: Entry level
Work Type: Other
Description: Job Opening Summary Under the direction of the RN, the ED technician facilitates patient care by providing advanced physical care and clinical procedures, providing patients with comfort measures and maintaining a clean and organized environment. Works as a member of the ED patient care team within the guidelines of his/her certification under the direct supervision of an ED physician. Job Opening Qualifications Education: Associate's degree preferred. Post-high school special training Licensure/Certification/Registration: Florida paramedic BLS ACLS preferred. Special Skills/Qualifications/Additional Training/Experience Required: Must be able to read, write in, speak and understand English.
